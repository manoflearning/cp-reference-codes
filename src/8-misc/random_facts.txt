GEOMETRY

45deg transform: Manhattan <-> Chebyshev on Z^2
  Setup: u = x + y, v = x - y.
  L1: |dx| + |dy| = max(|du|, |dv|).
  Lâˆž: max(|dx|, |dy|) = (|du| + |dv|) / 2.
  Use: max Manhattan over points = max( max(u)-min(u), max(v)-min(v) ).
  Note: du, dv have same parity -> (|du|+|dv|) is even -> /2 is integer.
  Why: max(|a+b|,|a-b|)=|a|+|b| and |a+b|+|a-b|=2max(|a|,|b|).

GRAPH

Complete bipartite K_{n,m}: number of spanning trees
  Formula: tau(K_{n,m}) = n^{m-1} * m^{n-1}.
  Why: Matrix-Tree theorem (Kirchhoff).
